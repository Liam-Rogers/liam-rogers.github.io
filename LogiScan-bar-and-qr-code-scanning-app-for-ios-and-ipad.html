About LogiScan

Turn your smartphone into a powerful inventory tool with LogiScan. Whether you're managing stock, organizing your pantry, or indexing items on the go, LogiScan makes it easy and fast!

Key Features

  • Fast Scanning: Simply point your phone at a Barcode or QR code, and the auto-detection process highlights the scanned code effortlessly.
  • Custom Data Entry: Assign your own locations, quantities, and best-before/expiry dates to items.
  • Export Made Simple: With one button, your accumulated data is turned into a formatted CSV/Excel file, ready for immediate use.

Designed to save you time and keep your inventory data accurate and accessible, LogiScan is the ultimate tool for both personal and professional use.
