MK6 Ford Escort Van - 2001 "55"

We are proud to present our FIRST ever widebody creation! Presenting the MK6 Escort Van, Combining the iconic RS2000 front conversion we all know and love with a handcrafted widebody kit. Custom lower splitter up front, Joined by wide front arches and custom wide body to the rear.

Suspension has been replaced for a flipped rear beam and coilovers at the front. Going back to the iconic look of the 90's and early 2000's we chose to use banded steels widened to 7J at the rear and 6 at the front.

Practicality may be questioned, But we can assure you this can still be used as a daily driver, Weather thats for picking up supplies from a builders merchant or the weekly shop. Theres no limit.
