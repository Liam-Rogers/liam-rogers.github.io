About SandC Kits

At SandC Kits, we are passionate about transforming vehicles into bold, unique masterpieces. Specializing in handcrafted wide body car kits, we help car enthusiasts push the limits of customization and design. Whether you're looking for an aggressive stance, wider track, or a completely bespoke appearance, we ensure every kit is designed to meet your specific needs and vision.

Our journey began in 2015 when two automotive engineers, Mark Seaton and Jane Collins, shared a vision to elevate the car customization scene. Drawing on years of industry expertise and a shared love for motorsports, they set out to design and create high-quality, hand-built wide body kits that would give vehicles both style and performance enhancements. Today, SandC Kits has become a trusted name in the automotive community, recognized for our craftsmanship, attention to detail, and commitment to quality.

Every kit we produce is crafted by hand in our UK workshop using the finest materials, ensuring that your vehicle not only stands out but is built to last. From concept to completion, we work closely with our customers to bring their dream builds to life.
